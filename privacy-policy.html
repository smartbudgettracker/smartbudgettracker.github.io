Privacy Policy

Moniro - Smart Money Tracker

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Moniro ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Moniro handles your information.

1. Offline First Application

Moniro is designed as an offline personal finance app. Your income, expenses, budgets, and categories are stored locally on your device.

2. No Account Required

You can use Moniro without creating an account. No login or registration is required.

3. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, sell, rent, or share your personal financial information except as necessary to provide specific app features described in this policy.

4. Local Device Storage

All core financial data remains on your device unless you manually export, sync, or back up your records.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent users' personal identification information to others. We only share limited data (including aggregated or anonymized data) with the following service providers to operate the app:

  • Third-Party AI Providers — Providers such as OpenAI may process your input data solely to deliver AI-powered features. Your data is not used to train their models.

6. Analytics & Crash Reports

We may use anonymous analytics or crash reporting tools to improve stability and user experience. These tools do not access your financial records unless required for diagnosing technical issues.

7. Third-Party Services

The app may be distributed through third-party platforms. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

8. Data Security

Since your data is stored locally, please secure your device with password, Face ID, Touch ID, or similar protection.

9. Children's Privacy

Moniro does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app works primarily offline and does not require account registration, personal data collection is limited to optional features described in this policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page.

11. Contact

If you have questions, please contact us through official support channels.
